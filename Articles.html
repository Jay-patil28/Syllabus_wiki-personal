Article 19

Right To Freedom From Articles 19-22 Clauses (a) to (e) & clause (g) of Article 19(1) guarantees to citizen of India six types of freedom, namely:

  1. Freedom of Speech & Expression
  2. Freedom of Assemble Peaceful & without Arms
  3. Freedom to form Association or Unions
  4. Freedom to Move Freely through the Territory of India
  5. Freedom to Reside & settle in any part of India
  6. Freedom to Practice any Profession or to carry out any occupation,trade or business.
