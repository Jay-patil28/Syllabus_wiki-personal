Super Profit Method

  • Needs
    • No of years of Purchase (g)
    • Super Profit
      • FMP [Capital Nominal/No of years]
      • Normal Profit
      • total Profit/Capital nominal
      • no of Years
      • Capital Employed (g/c)
      • NRR (g)
  • Main Formula

Goodwill = Super Profit x no of years

Capitalization of Super Profit/Avg Profit

  • Needs
    • Super/Avg Profit
    • NRR ( not in % )
      • FMP [Capital Nominal/No of years]
      • Normal Profit
      • total Profit/Capital nominal
      • no of Years
      • Capital Employed (g/c)
  • Main Formulas [Depends on Questions]
    1. Capitalization of Super Profit = 100/NRR x Super Profit
    2. Capitalization of Avg Profit = 100/NRR x Avg Profit

NRR is not in % eg 100/15 x 104

Intrinsic Value Method

This Method works on Assumption that Company is Liquidating

  • Needs
    • no of Equity Share Holders
  • Step-I 
    List out all assets [Market Value]
	+ include Goodwill
  • Step-II 
    Subtract Outside Liabilities From Total Assets [market value]
  • Step-III 
    Deduct Preference share [if any]
  • Step-IV 
    Calculate The Amount Available To equity share holders
	Assets
-	Liabilities
- Preference share
  • Step-V 
    Value of Share = Amount Available to equity shareholder/no of equity share

Yield Method

  • Needed
    • FMP
    • NRR
    • Expected Rate of Return
    • Paid up value per share
    • equity share capital
    • Profit Available of Equity share Holders
  • Step-I

Calculate FMP

  • Step-II

calculate Expected Rate of Return 

Expected Rate of Return = Profit available for equity share holders/equity share Capital x 100
  • Step-III

Calculate Value Per share 

Value Per share = Expected Rate of Return/Normal Rate of Return X Paid up value per share
