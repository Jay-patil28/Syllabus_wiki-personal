1) Difference Between Equity & Preference Share

Points of distinction Equity shares Preference shares
1) Payment of dividend Payment of Dividend is made after payment of Preference dividend. Preference Dividend is paid before payment of equity dividend.
2) Repayment Repayment of equity capital is made after repayment of Preference capital. Repayment of Preference capital is made before repayment of equity capital.
3) Rate if Dividend Rate of Dividend depends on the availability of Profit. rate of dividend is fixed.
4) Arrears of dividend Arrears of dividend cannot be accumulate. arrays of dividend may accumulate.
5) Convertibility It is not Convertible. It may be Convertible.
6) Redeemable Equity share are not redeemable during life time of the company. Preference share are Redeemable during life time of the company.
7) Voting Rights Shareholder enjoy voting rights. Shareholder do not have voting right except there class meetings.

2) Difference Between Share & Debentures

no Share Debentures
1. Share capital is the Proprietor's capital Debenture is a debt that is,Borrowed capital
2. Share holders are the owners of the company Debenture-holders are the creditors of the company
3. Dividend is paid to share holders only out of the Profit of the Company Debentures carry fixed rate of interest which has to be paid irrespective of the Profit or loss
4. Dividend to Shareholder is an appropriation out of the Profit Interest of Debentures is a charge against the Profit
5. In case of a winding up of the Company Shareholders have a last preference for repayment of capital In case of winding up of company, debenture-holders have priority over shareholders
