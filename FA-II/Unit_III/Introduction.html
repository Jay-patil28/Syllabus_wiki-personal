Meaning

  • Redemption means Repayment of capital. According to Section 61 of Companies Act 2014 No Company is Allowed to reduce or repay It's Capital, However as Per Sec 55 of Company's Act 2013 Company May Redeem or Repay it's Preference Share with certain Permission & Exception Section 55 also Says That the redemption Should not result into reduction of Capital.
  • Therefore, Redemption of preference share can be out of :-
    1. Proceeds of Fresh Issue of Shares
    2. Divisible Profit

Not form Chapter.-.

  • Divisible Profit & Non-Divisible Profit
    • Divisible Profit Means Which Are Available For Distribution of Dividend for eg.
      1. General Reserve
      2. Profit & Loss Account/ P&L a/c
      3. Dividend distribution Reserve
      4. Investment Fluctuation Reserve
    • Non-Divisible or Restricted Profit
      1. Capital Reserve
      2. Statutory Reserve
      3. Capital redemption Reserve
      4. Securities Premium
  • Section 55 also states that no company can redeem Preference Share unless or Until They are Fully Paid. Means Partly Paid Preference share Cannot be redeemed.
  • No Company can Issue Preference share for more then 20 years
