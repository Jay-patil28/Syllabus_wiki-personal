Procedure to take Life Insurance

  1. Proposal Form :: The Proposer who decides to take life insurance Policy makes Proposal through proposal form. This Form Provides relevant data for the purpose of risk assessment. The Proposal Form contains information like name,nationality,health,occupation,age,etc.
  2. Submission of Medical examination & Proof of age :: the Insurance premium is worked out on the basis of Age at the Entry. Medical Examination are also obtained but not in all cases, Depending upon age & sum assured
  3. Scrutiny & acceptance of Proposal :: The Proposal Form & Medical reports are examined by the office of the company, after scrutinising scrutiny of the Reports , the decision is Communicated to the Party & asked to do first payment of premium if the Proposal is Accepted .
  4. Issue of Policy :: after payment of first payment, Policy document are Issued this Policy document are Proof of Contract. The Policyholders has to keep this Documents under his custody.
