Advantages of Interviews

  1. They have all the advantages of face to face communication
  2. Non verbal cues – expressions, gestures, tone, eye movement, etc.
  3. Constant feedback
  4. Face to face interaction is the most effective way of evaluating a person

Disadvantages of Interviews

  1. Interviewer does not prepare properly
  2. Personal likes & dislike of the interviewer
  3. The mandatory Stress

Crisis Management

  1. Crisis can strike any organization, any time.
  2. In all crisis, a lot is at stake: image, reputation & money, at the individual as well as organizational level.
  3. Crisis Management may be defined as “taking decisions or finding solutions for crisis situations. It is the technique of managing crisis situations.
  4. There are crisis which are natural & they just happen. Then there are others which are man-made & can be avoided.

Advantages Disadvantages of Conference

Advantages of Conference

  1. Encourage exchange of ideas & opinions
  2. Analyzing problem
  3. Opportunity for face to face communication
  4. Has motivational value
  5. Helps create goodwill among organisations

Disadvantages of Conferences

  1. Informal in nature
  2. Might create confusion
  3. Domination
  4. Less authority
