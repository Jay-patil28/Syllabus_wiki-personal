Crisis Management

Crisis can strike any organization, any time. In all crisis, a lot is at stake: image, reputation & money, at the individual as well as organizational level.

Crisis Management may be defined as “taking decisions or finding solutions for crisis situations. It is the technique of managing crisis situations."

There are crisis which are natural & they just happen. Then there are others which are man-made & can be avoided.

  1. Sudden Market Shift
  2. Product Failure
  3. Top Management Succession
  4. Cash Crunch
  5. Industrial Relations
  6. Takeovers
  7. Adverse International Happening
  8. Wrong Public Perceptions

Advantages Disadvantages of Conference

Advantages of Conference

  1. Encourage exchange of ideas & opinions
  2. Brings out many facts of Problem & solution through discussion
  3. Analyzing problem & solution to determain next step
  4. Increase employee motivation with employee participation
  5. Opportunity for face to face communication
  6. Has motivational value
  7. Helps create goodwill among organisations

Disadvantages of Conferences

  1. Informal in nature
  2. if held often employees may feel management is unable to arrive at Decision on there on
  3. sometimes badly conduct conference create more confusion
  4. If person is not of Expertise to the topic he might feel inadequate

3 Advantages & Disadvantages of Interview

Advantages of Interviews

  1. They have all the advantages of face to face communication
  2. Non verbal cues – expressions, gestures, tone, eye movement, etc.
  3. Constant feedback
  4. Face to face interaction is the most effective way of evaluating a person

Disadvantages of Interviews

  1. Interviewer does not prepare properly
  2. Personal likes & dislike of the interviewer
  3. The mandatory Stress
