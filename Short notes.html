  • Natural Justice The Preamble of Constitution of India Includes words such as Justice,Social,economic & political which aiming towards the Principle of Natural Justice The Principle of Natural Justice have come to be because the need of a man to protect himself from excess of organized power Natural law can be seen common sense in Justice Natural Justice is an important part of Constitution of India Which can be seen in the Following articles that are 14,21,22,32,226,227,etc.
  • Principle of Natural Justice The main principle of Natural Justice are:-
    • No Man Shall be Judge in his own Cause the first principle of Natural Justice is That the judge should be Impartial. He Must Decide Impartially between the Parties that is He must be natural & free From Bias. The Judge Must not have any Personal or Direct In the subject matter of Enquiry This also Applies to all judicial & quasi Judicial Bodies
  • Both Parties to Be heard This Is second most Important Principle of Natural Justice this basically Implies Two things
    1. The Person who is to be condemned or Who's right are affected must have notice to Show cause way proposed proceeding should not be initiated against him
    2. he Must have Reasonable opportunity ti be heard

Thus right to being heard includes following rights:

  • Right to receive notice of proceedings
  • Right to oral hearing
  • Right to examine witness
  • Right to cross examination
  • Right to Explain things against one self This Rule basically means that both the Parties must be heart Before Passing any order
  • Specking order Every order must Contain reason in support of it, according to lord denning, the giving of Reasons is one of the fundamentals of good administration. It brings transparency, introduce clarity & avoids arbitrariness In Every case whether civil or criminal, Principle of Natural Justice Should be Followed.
