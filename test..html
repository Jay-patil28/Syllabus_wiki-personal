Procedure to Issue life insurance

  1. Proposal Form
    • proposer decides to take life insurance & make Proposal through Proposal for
    • contains relevant Information like age,name,health,occupation,etc.
  2. Submission of Proof of age & medical Examination
    • life Insurance premium is worked out on the basis of age at the entry
    • medical Examination are obtained but not in all cases, depending on age & sum assured
  3. scrutiny & Acceptance of Proposal form
    • the Proposal form & Medical Reports are reviewed by officer of the company
    • after the Scrutiny of reports
      • , the decision is Communicated to the Party And asked to pay first Premium (immediately) if Proposal is accepted
  4. Issue of Policy
    • if the first payment is done, the Policy Document is Issued
    • Policy Document is proof of Contract
    • The policyholder has to keep this Documents under his custody
